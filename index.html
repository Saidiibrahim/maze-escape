Game Features

Weapon Combat

Use your equipped weapon to eliminate enemies and destroy targets. Precise aiming and quick reflexes are essential for survival.

Experience immersive FPS controls with WASD movement and mouse look. Navigate tight corridors and open spaces with fluid motion.
